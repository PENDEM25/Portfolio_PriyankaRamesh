2. EDUmetrix: University management system
Sept 2023 – Nov 2023

This project presents an alternative viewpoint on the field of education. By shifting the attention from institutions to students and their instructors, it aims to solve some of the primary issues with traditional educational systems. this project has the potential to significantly transform education, especially in the areas of quality, price, and accessibility. This digital learning platform has broad potential effects: Accessibility, Affordability and Quality. Students are guaranteed a top-notch education through a reputation index for instructors. Furthermore, business involvement with the system, a third-party certification organization validating degrees, and permitting contributions from users by subscriptions also contribute to the platform's reputation, educational quality, and sustainability.
