About

Greetings! I'm Priyanka Ramesh, a versatile professional with a unique academic journey. I earned my bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the esteemed Global Academy of Technology. Following my undergraduate studies, I made a strategic pivot from electronics to information systems, fueled by a desire to explore the dynamic intersection of technology and business. This decision led me to pursue a Master's in Information Systems at Northeastern University, where I honed my skills in navigating the evolving landscape of digital solutions. The transition allowed me to broaden my perspective and adapt to the rapidly changing tech industry. Embracing the limitless potential of web design, I was drawn to the profession by its transformative ability to bridge creativity and functionality. The power to craft immersive online experiences and contribute to the digital evolution of businesses became my compelling motivation. Today, as a dedicated web designer, I channel my passion into creating impactful and user-centric designs that resonate with the modern demands of the digital era.
